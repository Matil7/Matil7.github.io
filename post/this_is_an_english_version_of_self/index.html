product part

PACKAGING

As the nondurable goods,also the hedonic goods,

The core of Weilong brand positioning lies in “health, delicious, fashion”. Moreover, Weilong’s mission “making everyone in the world fall in love with Chinese taste”.

In order to make “healthy” spicy noodle products, from the beginning of its establishment, Weilong Spicy strip has taken product quality as the primary task of its development,with high quality raw materials, advanced production technology and strict quality control, to ensure the health and delicious products.

Raw materials,selects flour from northern latitude Golden Wheat region, first-grade soybean oil, Gansu Wudu pepper, Xinjiang cumin and other seasonings.

Production technology,Weilong adheres to the concept of less original processing, and uses non-fried methods to allow consumers to eat more at ease.

UNIQUENESS

Weilong’s “health” is take the lead in opening the road to automated and intelligent modern production. Through a series of leading digital technologies in the industry, Weilong not only promotes the safe production of products, but also significantly improves production efficiency and effectively reduces production costs and labor costs. Weilong’s strict selection of raw materials, production process control and the establishment of quality inspection system ensure that the safety and taste of Weilong products have been maintained in the industry’s leading level, won the trust and reputation of consumers, and set a new production benchmark for the industry.

PLACE STRATGY

Location advantage:

Henan: (nutural edge)The production base is located in densely populted areas, which is conducive to reducing transportation costs and improving sales efficiency.

(human edge)Local governments pay attention to supporting the development of enterprises by improving the innovation chain and the system chain.

Weilong Group has always been adhering to the procurement of agricultural products of origin, and has signed purchase orders with many cooperatives, committed to promoting the development of rural economy, and truly providing high-quality, convenient and efficient product sales for farmers and agriculture. more professional, scientific to help more families increase earnings.

Factories located near China’s pepper region ,so provide easier access to fresh pepper ingredients. The flour（面粉） is provided by COFCO noodle industry in the Jingkai District, the capsaicin is provided by Zhongda Hengyuan of Linying County, and the salt is provided by Wuyang in Wuyang County. Only the food materials such as pepper, konjac powder and cumin are purchased from other places, and supported by the developed cold chain logistics in dustry in the area, so the transportation cost is low.

Policy advantages:

(Advantages of the park) is supported by relevant government policies, financial support, tax relief or preferential treatment, industry supervision and other aspects. Luohe Economic Development Zone took the initiative to provide convenient conditions, and large food enterprises settled in Weilong First Development Zone at the same time.
