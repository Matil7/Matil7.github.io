This is an English version of self-introduction

Hello, friends who are willing to browse this website, here is Yangyang, Matilda, a 20-year-old who has not thought about where to go next, from Changsha, Hunan, that can eat spicy place, now living in the island of Macau, although not love to eat Cantonese food, but particularly want to learn Cantonese.

I like water, like swimming, also like surfing, the sea always gives me a comfortable and inclusive feeling, if one day I will be buried here I also enjoy it.

There is always something that will make you feel worthy of all the troubles and enthusiasm, in addition to the carrier of water, music is also the most important, I love music, whether it is in the form of elegant classical piano, energetic street dance, even the identity of the lead singer, whenever those melodies sound, I know I will pay all for it. Every time I rehearse with the band members, every roar and rhythm makes me forget myself. As peers, we have so much to share and talk about. Best wishes to Escherichee, Jun, 14, Apa, and Tail. If you want to hear this terrible band you can stop by and buy a drink.

I have a super fun brother, but I must have been a cool older sister to him at 13, I hope, but it’s so nice to have him now, time has finally turned us into adults who can do whatever they want, those little kids' days will never fade, and my family’s two big heads, I like them best when they give me money.

I like freedom, like casual, like unfettered, like to travel, I like everything I have now, I feel very happy and full, sometimes to the far life of those happiness may not do anything, just have those pleasant mood, family peace, friends peace, this may be the greatest happiness, hope to read here people can be happy and safe
